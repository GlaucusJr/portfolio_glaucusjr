About Me

Hello! My name is Glaucus Junior, and I am a Full Stack Developer passionate about turning ideas into efficient, modern, and functional digital solutions. With training from Cubos Academy and ongoing Software Engineering studies, I have been building projects that combine performance, aesthetics, and great user experience.

Throughout my journey, I have developed complete web applications, handling both front-end and back-end, with expertise in technologies such as JavaScript, TypeScript, Node.js, React, Next.js, HTML, CSS, Postgres, Docker, and testing with Jest. I also have experience with Git version control and creating interfaces based on Figma prototypes.

I have an eye for detail, organization, and focus on delivering value. I enjoy writing clean code, continuously learning, and collaborating in multidisciplinary teams. Each project in my portfolio is an opportunity to demonstrate how I can contribute to solving real problems with technology.

If you are looking for someone committed, with good communication skills, who knows how to combine technical expertise with creativity, whether to join your team or develop a product from scratch, it will be a pleasure to talk!
